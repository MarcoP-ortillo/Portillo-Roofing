Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our roofing services.

How quickly can you respond to emergencies?

We respond to emergency calls within 2 hours. For critical situations like active leaks or storm damage, we provide same-day temporary repairs to prevent further damage.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer flexible financing options for qualified customers. We can discuss payment plans during your free consultation to find a solution that works for your budget.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. We are fully licensed, bonded, and insured for your protection. We can provide proof of insurance and licensing upon request.

How long do roofing projects typically take?

Most residential roofing projects are completed in 1-3 days, depending on the size and complexity. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Do you help with insurance claims?

Yes, we have extensive experience working with insurance companies. We can help document damage, provide detailed reports, and work directly with your adjuster.

What warranties do you offer?

We offer comprehensive warranties including manufacturer warranties on materials and our own workmanship guarantee. Specific terms vary by product and service.
