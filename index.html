Why Choose Portillo Roofing?

Portillo Roofing LLC was founded with a clear mission: to bring honesty, craftsmanship, and fairness back to the roofing industry. After 25+ years of experience, we understand that your roof is more than just protection—it's an investment in your home's future and your family's safety.

Led by Marco Portillo Sr. and his son, our multigenerational team combines time-tested experience with fresh energy and relentless attention to detail. We specialize in metal roofing but are highly experienced in all roofing systems.

We're not just here to put a roof over your head—we're here to earn your trust, build lasting relationships, and raise the bar for what a roofing company should be.
