About Us

Portillo Roofing LLC was founded with a clear purpose: to bring honesty, craftsmanship, and fairness back to the roofing industry. After more than 25 years of subcontracting for a large company known for overcharging clients and underpaying workers, Marco Portillo Sr. knew there had to be a better way — one built on respect, integrity, and community values.

Driven by that vision, Marco Sr. teamed up with his son to create a roofing company where quality work and ethical service go hand in hand. Together, we bring a multigenerational approach to the trade, blending time-tested experience with fresh energy and relentless attention to detail.

We specialize in metal roofing, but our team is well-equipped and highly experienced in all roofing systems — from composition shingles to commercial-grade flat roofs. Whether you need a full replacement, repair, or honest guidance, we approach every job with care, precision, and open communication.

We’re not just here to put a roof over your head — we’re here to earn your trust, build lasting relationships, and raise the bar for what a roofing company should be.
