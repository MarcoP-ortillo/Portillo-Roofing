Roof Replacement

Whether your existing roof has seen better days or suffered storm damage, a full roof replacement gives you a solid foundation you can rely on for decades. Choose the option that best fits your goals and budget:

Premium Metal Replacement

We remove your old roof down to the deck and install a standing‑seam metal system that delivers:

  • Exceptional Longevity: Lasts 40–50 years or more, backed by industry‑leading warranties.
  • Superior Durability: Resists hail, wind, fire and UV damage far better than asphalt.
  • Energy Savings: Reflective coatings cut cooling bills by 20–25 %.
  • Minimal Maintenance: No more cracked or curling shingles to replace.

Shingle Replacement

For a budget‑friendly option, we’ll strip off your old shingles and underlayment, then install premium architectural or three‑tab shingles that provide:

  • Affordability: Lower upfront cost with quality materials and a 15–25 year warranty.
  • Speedy Installation: Get your new roof in place faster, minimizing disruption.
  • Reliable Protection: Engineered to stand up to wind, rain and UV exposure.
